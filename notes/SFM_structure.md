# SFM_structure

$$
\begin{aligned}
Q_x &= F(K_x, L_x)   \\
Q_y &= G(K_y, L_y)    \\
\bar L &= L_x + L_y  \\
K_x &= \bar K_x \\
K_y &= \bar K_y
\end{aligned}
$$
